My Journey

My path to becoming a design strategist wasn't linear—it was a deliberate evolution shaped by curiosity, diverse experiences, and an unwavering belief that great design changes lives.

I started my career in traditional graphic design, creating beautiful visuals for print campaigns. But I quickly realized that aesthetics alone weren't enough. I wanted to understand the "why" behind every design decision and measure its impact on business outcomes.

This curiosity led me to pursue formal training in UX design, business strategy, and behavioral psychology. I spent years working with startups, Fortune 500 companies, and everything in between—learning that the best design solutions emerge from deep empathy, rigorous research, and collaborative problem-solving. Each project taught me something new about human behavior, market dynamics, and the delicate balance between innovation and usability.

Today, I approach every project as a strategic partnership. I don't just execute briefs—I challenge assumptions, ask difficult questions, and work alongside clients to uncover opportunities they didn't know existed. My process combines data-driven insights with creative intuition, ensuring that every design decision is both beautiful and purposeful.

Beyond client work, I'm passionate about elevating the design profession. I speak at industry conferences, mentor emerging designers, and contribute thought leadership that bridges design and business strategy. This portfolio isn't just a showcase of projects—it's a testament to the transformative power of design thinking when applied with intention, expertise, and genuine care for the people we serve.
