Project Overview

U-List is a modern music streaming platform designed to simplify playlist creation and sharing across multiple services. Users wanted a seamless way to manage playlists and share them with friends from and to Spotify without friction. We created an intuitive mobile app that bridges the gap between different music platforms and puts playlist discovery and sharing at the forefront.

The Challenge

Fragmented Playlist Management

Users had to manage playlists across multiple platforms without a unified view or control.

Complex Sharing Experience

Sharing playlists between Spotify and other services was convoluted and lacked proper integration.

Poor Discovery Features

Finding and accessing shared playlists from friends was difficult and not intuitive.

Limited Customization

Users couldn't easily personalize their music experience across platforms.

Our Solution

We designed a music-first experience that simplifies playlist management and makes sharing intuitive through seamless Spotify integration.

Unified Playlist Hub

Centralized dashboard for managing all playlists from multiple sources in one place.

One-Tap Spotify Sync

Effortless synchronization and sharing of playlists directly to and from Spotify accounts.

Smart Discovery Feed

Personalized recommendations and friend-shared playlists surfaced intelligently.

Collaborative Playlist Tools

Real-time collaboration features allowing friends to add songs and manage playlists together.
