The Babel Edit

Transforming a fashion retail platform with strategic UX design that increased conversion rates by 156%

Client The Babel Edit
Industry Fashion Retail
Duration 6 Months
Services UX/UI Design, Conversion Optimization

Project Overview

The Babel Edit approached us with a struggling e-commerce platform that was losing customers at every touchpoint. Their existing site had high bounce rates, low conversion, and poor user experience that didn't reflect their premium fashion brand. We embarked on a comprehensive redesign that transformed their digital presence and business metrics.

The Challenge

High Cart Abandonment

78% of users were abandoning their carts before checkout, costing the business thousands in lost revenue.

Poor Mobile Experience

The site wasn't optimized for mobile, where 65% of their traffic originated, leading to frustrated users.

Complex Navigation

Users couldn't easily find products, leading to high bounce rates and low engagement.

Trust Issues

Lack of social proof and unclear shipping/policies eroded customer confidence.

Our Solution

We implemented a user-centered design approach that prioritized conversion optimization while maintaining the brand's premium aesthetic.

Streamlined User Journey

Reduced checkout steps from 7 to 3, implementing guest checkout and progress indicators.

Mobile-First Design

Complete responsive redesign with touch-optimized interactions and improved loading speeds.

Enhanced Product Discovery

Advanced filtering, search, and recommendation system that increased product views by 40%.

Trust-Building Elements

Added customer reviews, trust badges, and clear policies to build confidence.
