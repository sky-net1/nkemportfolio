Project Overview

HEINENKEN Educational Foundation is a mission-driven NGO dedicated to supporting students through multiple channels. The foundation provides scholarships, food assistance, palliatives, and student loans to help underprivileged students achieve their educational goals. We designed a comprehensive digital platform to streamline program management, improve accessibility, and maximize the foundation's impact on student communities.

The Challenge

Fragmented Application Process

Students struggled with multiple separate application forms for scholarships, food aid, and loans.

Limited Reach and Awareness

Many deserving students didn't know about available programs and support services.

Manual Program Management

Processing applications and distributing aid was time-consuming and error-prone.

Lack of Student Engagement

Limited feedback mechanisms to track student outcomes and improve program effectiveness.

Our Solution

We designed an integrated digital platform that unifies all foundation services while prioritizing ease of use for students from diverse backgrounds.

Unified Application Portal

Single platform for scholarship applications, food assistance requests, palliative programs, and student loans.

Student-Friendly Interface

Simple navigation designed for students with varying technical skills and device access.

Real-Time Tracking Dashboard

Students can monitor application status, disbursement schedules, and program benefits in one place.

Impact Measurement System

Foundation staff can track outcomes, generate impact reports, and optimize program delivery.
